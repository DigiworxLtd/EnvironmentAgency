What's the problem we're trying to solve and how does this analysis help?

The Floodline service re-tender (2018) has led the Environment Agency to consider how digital channels could be used to support the service in the future. The analysis displayed here provides insight into the usage of Floodline which we've used to inform recommendations of where we think digital can support the service.

The analysis completed is based on data sets collected between November 2015 and October 2017.
