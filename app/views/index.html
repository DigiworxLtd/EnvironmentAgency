sdWhats the problem we are trying to solve and how does this analysis helps?

The Floodline service re-tender (2018) has led the Environment Agency to consider how digital channels could be used to support the service in the future. The analysis displayed on this page provides insight into the usage of floodline which we've used to inform reccomendations of where digital can support the delivery of floodline.

The analysis completed is based on data sets collected between November 2015 and October 2017. All data sets are available here - need to add link

2.0 Why calls are transferred away from floodline

Reporting flooding and possible causes of flooding represent a significant proportion of the calls transferred away from Floodline.

3.0 Outcomes of calls fielded by Floodline

A significant amount of calls are related to the user administration of FWS i.e. check registration details, request registration or cancellations.
